What we collect

This is a static research and information site. We do not ask you to create an account and we do not collect names, email addresses, or payment details through the site itself. The only personal data we process is analytics data, and only if you consent.

Cookies and Google Analytics

With your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 (provided by Google) to understand, in aggregate, how visitors use the site, so we can improve it. When you accept, Google Analytics sets cookies and processes data such as:

  • pages and demos you view, and the order you view them in;
  • approximate location (country/region, derived from your IP address, which Google Analytics 4 does not store);
  • device, browser, and screen information;
  • referring site and a randomly generated identifier used to recognise a returning browser.

No analytics cookies are set until you click Accept on the banner. Until then, Google Analytics runs in a restricted, cookieless mode (Google Consent Mode v2) with advertising signals disabled. If you click Decline, analytics stays off.
