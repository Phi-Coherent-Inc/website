What this demo claims — and what it does not. A Fibonacci anyon has quantum dimension exactly the golden ratio (d_τ = φ, the root of τ × τ = 1 + τ), which makes it the minimal anyon theory universal by braiding alone, with no magic-state distillation. That is the one place the golden ratio is genuinely load-bearing in quantum error correction. It does NOT claim:
  • that Fibonacci-anyon QEC beats the surface code (+ distillation) on threshold or overhead — it does not; the off-φ Ising comparator wins the system axis (cheap poly-time decoder + amortizable 15-to-1 distillation);
  • that the threshold shown is scalable or independently validated — it is a small-instance code-capacity surrogate; the true non-Abelian (string-net) decoder is #P-flavoured and BQP-complete to validate;
  • that Fibonacci anyons are realized hardware — this is simulation, computed live from phi-quantum-topoqec-cpp via WebAssembly.
