Conversation

Senses (optional)

The default experience is chat, and chat needs no permissions. Each granted sense is wired to the mind's own trained faculties, live: the camera feeds frames through its trained visual encoder every few seconds and it may comment on what it sees; the microphone feeds sound windows through its trained hearing encoder; the speaker plays the mind's own voice, a vocalization decoded from its response state by its trained speech decoder (not text-to-speech). Sensing takes real thought: a glance costs seconds, a listen tens of seconds, so the mind samples its senses rather than streaming them. Deny everything and nothing is lost: the conversation above is the complete baseline.
